Feeling “behind” or scared to start is one of the biggest blocks for students in Years 7 to 10.

In this post, we look at three gentle ways you can support your teenager:

1. Start with one small, clear win each afternoon.
2. Use checklists instead of long to-do lists.
3. Focus on effort and strategies, not just marks.
