Comprehension & fluency notes Tutor only

Suggested comprehension answers:

  • Glitter trail (Passage 1): It starts near the front door and ends at the Christmas tree.
  • Under the tree (Passage 1): A big box wrapped in green paper with a silver bow.
  • Reason for glitter trail (Passage 1): To lead Mia to a special present / surprise and build excitement.
  • Sound that woke Tom (Passage 2): A soft tapping on his window.
  • ‚ÄúShine‚Äù (Passage 2): Suggests that the snow looked bright, glowing or sparkling in the light.
  • ‚ÄúWorld is waiting for Christmas‚Äù (Passage 2): The quiet, snowy scene feels calm and still, as if everything is ready and just waiting for Christmas to begin.

Fluency focus: praise self-corrections, attention to punctuation, and any growth in smoothness between Read 1 and Read 3, not just raw speed.
